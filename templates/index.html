Frequently Asked Question

Find answers to the most common questions about our project management tool.
What is Helpo?

Helpo is an online platform where students can ask questions, and experienced teachers provide clear, detailed answers to help with their academic challenges.

How does Helpo work?

Simply sign up, post your question, and one of our expert teachers will provide an answer tailored to your needs. You can track your questions and receive notifications when your questions are answered.

How long does it take to get an answer?

Our teachers strive to answer questions as quickly as possible, usually within 24 hours. Response times may vary depending on the complexity of the question.

Is Helpo free to use?

Helpo offers both free and premium services. Basic questions can be asked for free, while more detailed or complex questions may require a premium subscription.

What subjects can I ask about?

Helpo covers a wide range of subjects, including math, science, history, literature, and more. Our diverse team of teachers is ready to assist with any academic question.

Can I get personalized help for my assignments?

Yes, our teachers provide personalized assistance tailored to your specific needs, whether it’s for homework, exam preparation, or general understanding.

How do I communicate with the teacher?

You can communicate directly with your teacher through our messaging system on Helpo. Ask follow-up questions or request further clarification as needed.

Is my information secure on Helpo?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. All your data and communications are secure and confidential on our platform.
